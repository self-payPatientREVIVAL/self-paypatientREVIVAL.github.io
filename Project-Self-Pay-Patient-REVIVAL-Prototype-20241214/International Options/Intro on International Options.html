As mentioned in the homepage, Mr. Pranell's groundbreaking work focused on a menu of solutions for cutting down the cost of healthcare expenses here in the U.S., and only mentioned the rest of the world in terms of medical-tourism. But, how about the rest of the world? Surely, the people in the States aren't alone in the fight for lower medical-bills, right? That's why I'm also expanding this to also cover options worldwide!

God willing, all will be able to find solutions with this listing: Locals across the globe, travellers (for business and/or for pleasure) that suddenly require medical-services, and/or medical-tourists traveling ESPECIALLY for lower-priced and/or higher-quality healthcare, etc. Let's see!
